FAQ'S

Q: What is E-Library?

This is an Open-Source Library Website in which you get Resources to learn different topics, Add book section to add your e-books, and a Book issue section to keep a record of all the books issued.

Q: What is the best part of E-Library?

The best part about the website is that you can also contribute to the website for others to use the resourses by adding the books you have, all categories of books are welcomed for addition.

Q: How to become a member?

By providing link of any book of your choice in expand E-Library page, you can become a member of the E-Library.

Q: What is the registration fee?

There is no registration fees! One simply needs to adding a book to become a member.

Q: I can't find the book I am looking for. What to do?

The library has a huge collection of books of multiple genres. However, if you can't find the book you're looking for, do reach out to us! Drop us a message through the "Contact Us" section.

Q: How can I add a book?

Click on the "Add books" button and fill out the form with your details. We will add that book within few days.

Q: How can I find a book here?

Go to the "Choose and read books" section and search the book you are looking for .

Q: I haven't found a book but it's not readable format ? Is something wrong ?

This is not supposed to happen but if it happens please leave a message on our "Contact us" section. We will definitely look in it.

Q: How can I download a book?

Go to our "Recommended Book" section. Choose the book which you want to download then click on the "Download as pdf". Happy reading :)

Q: How many books can I checkout at once?

You can checkout as many books as you want.

Q: How can I access in Online library?

Go to our "Resources" section.Click on the "All resources". You can find out in our environment all recources to satisfy your need.
